# Founding Engineer Hiring Plan (May 2026)

## Scope
- Role: Founding Full-Stack Engineer (Travel Product)
- Hiring owner: CTO (agent `acf51b26-539d-4e24-a901-384cc8df20d0`)
- Interviewing panel owner: CTO until additional engineering interviewers are onboarded
- Target start window: June 2026

## Role Definition
- Seniority: Senior+ individual contributor with startup execution range
- Core mission (first 90 days):
  - Ship monetized travel recommendation UX improvements tied to affiliate conversion events.
  - Improve delivery velocity with pragmatic full-stack architecture and testing standards.
  - Co-own technical planning from product idea to production launch.
- Must-have background:
  - Full-stack production delivery (frontend + backend + data integrations).
  - Strong product judgment with evidence of shipping customer-facing features.
  - Comfortable with ambiguity, scoping, and tradeoff decisions.
- Nice-to-have:
  - Travel, marketplaces, content/recommendation system, or affiliate monetization exposure.
  - Early-stage or founding-team experience.

## Hiring Scorecard (100 points)
- Technical execution (35): ships reliable code quickly; debugging depth; architecture tradeoffs.
- Product sense (25): prioritization, UX pragmatism, understanding conversion funnels.
- Ownership and autonomy (20): drives outcomes without heavy process overhead.
- Communication and collaboration (10): clear written/spoken reasoning across functions.
- Startup fit and pace (10): bias to action, resilience, comfort with uncertainty.

## Interview Loop
1. Recruiter/CTO screen (30 min)
- Validate motivation, compensation band, location/time-zone fit, startup expectations.
- Gate: proceed only if candidate demonstrates explicit interest in early-stage ownership.

2. Technical deep dive (60 min)
- Candidate walks through one shipped system and one incident/debug scenario.
- Gate: must demonstrate clear tradeoff reasoning and operational competence.

3. Product + execution case (60 min)
- Prompt: prioritize a 2-week roadmap to improve affiliate conversion on a travel recommendation page.
- Gate: must show structured prioritization and measurable success metrics.

4. Live build / pairing (90 min)
- Build a scoped full-stack feature slice with tests and instrumentation discussion.
- Gate: coding quality, iteration speed, and communication while implementing.

5. Founder/CEO alignment (45 min)
- Assess mission alignment and long-term collaboration fit.
- Final decision meeting uses scorecard averages + written debriefs.

## Risks and Mitigations
- Risk: Candidate quality too broad from generic outbound.
  - Mitigation: tighten sourcing to evidence-based profiles (recent shipped product work, startup tenure).
- Risk: Process drag from unclear evaluation criteria.
  - Mitigation: use fixed scorecard with written debrief requirement at each stage.
- Risk: Compensation mismatch late in pipeline.
  - Mitigation: discuss comp expectations in first screen and maintain transparent band early.
- Risk: Single-interviewer bottleneck while team is small.
  - Mitigation: block weekly interview windows on calendar and standardize rubrics to reduce prep cost.

## Success Criteria for This Loop
- At least 30 candidates sourced.
- At least 30 outbound messages sent.
- At least 5 completed screens.
- At least 2 candidates advanced into technical deep dive.
